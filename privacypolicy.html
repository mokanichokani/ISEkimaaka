Privacy Policy for ISEkimaaka

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of information for the "ISEkimaaka" Chrome extension.

1. No Collection of Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). This extension does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, password, or any other personal information.

2. Collection of Website Content

The core function of this extension requires capturing an image of your active browser tab on any website. This action is performed only when you explicitly trigger it.

  • The captured image is sent to the Google Gemini API for the sole purpose of performing the requested AI analysis.
  • This image data is processed ephemerally and is not stored or logged on our servers or by the extension after the analysis is complete.

3. Data Sharing

We share the captured image data with Google LLC to use their Gemini API. We do not share any other data. Google's use of this information is governed by its own privacy policies.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the extension's store listing.
